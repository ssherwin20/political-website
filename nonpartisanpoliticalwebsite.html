Trump vs Hillary

The presidential candidates in 2016 are Donald Trump and Hillary Clinton. They both have very different stances on the issues in our country and how to handle those issues. On this website, you will easily be able to see both of their stances on these issues and you will be able to learn a little bit more about the candidates.


Donald Trump is the leading candidate for the Republican party. He is 70 years old and is a resident in Manhattan, New York. He is a business owner and a politician and he has a net worth of 4.5 billion US dollars as of July 2016. Trump announced that he was running for president on June 6, 2015 and he quickly became the leading Republican candidate. Hillary Clinton is the leading candidate for the Democratic party. Her husband, Bill Clinton, was a former president of the United States (1992-1996). In 1999, she obtained a seat as the US Senate from New York. She then got nominated for the US Secretary of State by Barack Obama, which she accepted. She announced that she was running for president on April 12, 2016.

Hillary Clinton's Stance on Issues in the US

  1. Death Penalty should be allowed.
  2. The United States shouldn't continue the war on drugs.
  3. There shouldn't be a flat tax on income.
  4. Social Security shouldn't be privatized.
  5. Some public colleges or universities should be tuition-free.
  6. China is an economic and military threat to the United States.
  7. Abortion is a woman's rights and it should be allowed till the moment of labor.
  8. People who are on the no-fly list or the terror watch list shouldn't be able to purchase a gun.
  9. More gun control laws should be enacted.
  10. No comment on whether the US should continue to build the fence/wall along the US-Mexico border.
  11. Undocumented immigrants should be allowed to become legal residents of the US.
  12. The federal minimum wage should be increased.
  13. The US shouldn't send troops out to fight against ISIS.
